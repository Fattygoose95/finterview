## 🎯 Why finterview?

Finance interviews are make‑or‑break moments that separate top candidates from the rest. Traditional preparation methods are outdated, inefficient, and fail to simulate real‑world pressure.

**finterview revolutionizes interview prep with:**
- **AI‑Driven Intelligence** – Advanced algorithms analyze thousands of interview patterns to predict what you'll actually be asked
- **1000+ Expert‑Curated Questions** – The largest database of finance interview questions across 8 major industries
- **Real‑Time Interview Simulation** – Practice with our AI agent that adapts to your performance and provides instant feedback
- **Bloomberg‑Grade Interface** – Professional trading‑floor design that trains you to think like a finance professional
- **Competitive Edge Analytics** – Benchmark your performance against thousands of successful candidates

Built by finance experts and AI engineers, finterview is the platform that top candidates use to land roles at Goldman Sachs, J.P. Morgan, BlackRock, and other elite firms.

- **Personalized question recommendations** based on your performance and target roles

### 2. **Adaptive Practice System**
- **Intelligent difficulty scaling** that matches your skill level and progression

### 3. **Bloomberg‑Grade Interface**

```text

│   ├── css/                      # Design system + components
```
